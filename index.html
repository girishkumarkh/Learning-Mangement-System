Learning Management System

LMSs are important wrappers for systems such as surgical and medical simulators to allow trainees to work and progress in an unsupervised manner. This prototype LMS uses suitable tools to act as the front end of a endoscopic simulator presently under development in collaboration with colleagues in Anaesthesia at the Heath Hospital. The work will involve researching objective metrics to provide formative and summative feedback and to score trainees' performance.
